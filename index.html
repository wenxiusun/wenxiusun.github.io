Biography

I received the Ph.D. degree from the Electronic and Computer Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2014, and B.Eng. degree from the School of Electronic & Electrical Engineering, Nanjing University in 2009. During my Ph.D. study from Sep. 2009 to Mar. 2014, I was supervised by Prof. Oscar C. Au. From Sep. to Dec. 2012, I visited National Institute of Informatics (NII) as a research intern under the supervision of Prof. Gene Cheung.

I'm interested in large language models and computer vision. In particular, I worked on depth sensing, segmentation, and image processing to solve real-world problems. Currently, I'm focusing on LLM evaluation and development to advance the progress of the next AGI.

I am currently working at SenseTime as a Research Director and recruiting self-motivated researchers and engineers who are eager and patient to make contributions to the world. If interested, please drop your CV to my email.
